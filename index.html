My name is Le Ngoc Phuong Trinh - a Bachelor of International Business Economics at Foreign Trade University HCMC.
Within a year of graduation, I had through several changes in my career path, moving from International Transportation to Supply Chain, and now focusing on Data Analysis and Working Automation, and eventually becoming a Product Owner. Despite the fact that my domain is economics rather than technical, I have a great interest in Data Analytics and Programming Languages.
Growing as a self-learner at the beginning, I am now capable of using Python, SQL, VBA, Power Query, Power BI. I'm always looking for new ways to better my abilities and learn new things, such as Tableau or Google Data Studio. I'd like to be able to adapt to any working environment with any working platform.
Because I place a high value on aesthetics, every product I produce must be attractive and good-looking. Perhaps this contributes my interest in Data Storytelling strategies.
